thermo command :h3

[Syntax:]

thermo N :pre

N = output thermodynamics every N timesteps :ul

[Examples:]

thermo 100 :pre

[Description:]

Compute and print thermodynamics (temperature, energy, pressure) on
timesteps that are a multiple of N and at the beginning and end of a
simulation.  A value of 0 will only print thermodynamics at the
beginning and end.

[Restrictions:] none

[Related commands:]

"thermo_style"_thermo_style.html, "thermo_modify"_thermo_modify.html

[Default:]

thermo 0 :pre
